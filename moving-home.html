The Ultimate Moving House Checklist

Author:Ray Vel Last Updated: May 24, 2022

Sudden planning of house moving can be fearful but you don't need to worry because to ease from this problem our company is created a complete list and preparing you for the moving day. You are doing right to check this we make a checklist so that everything will be well organized whether moving or packing and you will be stress-free. We will make a list of the 8 weeks for the whole schedule. In case of any mishaps or unexpected problem will arise then our expert team of house removal will help you to solve the problem.

If you want to remember your track you can also download our free PDF checklist and mark the work done and what is left. You can paste the printout of pdf on your fridge and work accordingly

TWO MONTHS TO GO BEFORE MOVING HOUSE

  • Collect all important documents and paper and keep them in the folder so that they cannot be a miss or lost during the home removal process.
  • With the help of shopping, recreational activities and local areas research the new area. Check the location whether it is near to tube station or far from Central London
  • Search new schools and order new uniforms of school, if you have children.
  • Give notice about your scheduled moving date to your landlord if you are on rent.

SIX-EIGHT WEEKS TO GO

  • Now start to remove your items. You can donate or sell all the items which you don't require anymore. If you want to donate your items so in North London there are many funding shops where you can give all items which you did not use for many years and don't require anymore.
  • Make a catalog of all your belongings.
  • To get an accurate price start to contact different moving companies. Click here for a free quote!
  • For your convenience, we are also providing a packing service but if you are packing by yourself so start packing your materials and store them
  • Just make an idea that which items you are taking with you and where you will put that item. Use the idea of a clear floor plan so you will know where everything has to go.
  • For removal of large vehicles please check out the parking rules and their restriction on your current location as well as your new home. If you need any guidance about parking or loading instruction please click on the link.

  • Verify with your removal company about your moving date.
  • Think about whether you’ll be needing self-storage. We offer competitive rates with our storage partners in London, so get in touch for a quote.
  • Now let's start packing your items firstly start with those items which are often used and don't forget to label the boxes so you don't have a problem while unboxing your belongings.

  • Cleaning a home is one of the difficult tasks during home moving while your previous home owner goes out and you are moving in. 2-3 WEEKS TO GO

  • Pack every rest utensils of your house, and use good packing material for the protection of furniture.
  • All those items which you needed for the first night make a list of those items don't pack them yet
  • Inform all the people you want about changing your address. Make a list so that you don't miss any person. Further included.
  • HMRC
  • DVLA
  • TV
  • Electoral Roll
  • Licensing
  • Financial institutions: lenders, banks, and credit card companies
  • Water, Gas, and electricity suppliers.
  • Insurance providers: home insurance, car insurance, home insurance, life insurance, health insurance.
  • Your regular bills such as home and mobile phone, broadband, and TV
  • Contact the people on your work such as your employers, professional connections, and if you have children then educational institutions numbers.
  • Health services like dentists, opticians and GP surgery  
  • Any recreational companies: gyms, magazines, and newspaper subscriptions.
  • Your pet’s microchip details.
  • To redirect all your mail use a redirect service and update all your details. Alternatively, you can also use the Post Office’s change-of-address service for your mail as it is available in London and its surrounding areas as well as in the UK.
  • With the team of movers and packers please confirm your details I.e timings, parking, direction, and emergency number. In London there are many working routes so decide your route before moving.
  • You have to register yourself for council tax and pay for it in your new area. Search your local council on GOV.UK.

ONE WEEK TO GO

  • Systemically pack every item as soon as possible and done with your packing
  • Freezer food should be frozen down and done with perishables food.
  • If you borrow any item from another and forget to return it to another so return that.
  • Deposit enough amount in any bank that in any case of emergency you will not be in trouble.
  • Schedule a date and time to collect your new home’s key. If you have any pets keep them in any other place for one day. Also, arrange an appropriately sized carrier for your pet.
  • For overnight pack a bag having trousers, chargers for every house member, and toiletries so that there will be no problem for you during transportation and no need to open any box.
  • Decide a time and day for which family members will reach out to you for help and at what time the movers will arrive at you.

1 DAY TO GO

  • When 1 day is left to move to go through your house at least at once that is nothing important you left behind before moving.
  • Confirm that you collect the keys to your new home and organized them.
  • Keep all the keys to the locks of the old home together.
  • Be chill and take a good sleep.

ON DAY OF YOUR HOUSE MOVE

Don't worry you will enjoy this experience as it is a stress-free experience just take everything one step at a time. At the given scheduled time your allotted removal company will be there for you.

  • When one day left then fastly pack all your bedding and curtains.
  • In case any moving company is hired by you so make sure that house removal team members will be there at your location at the given time. One member of the family is also being there so there is no item left behind in the old home.
  • If you have any special instructions please inform your movers and also tell them where all boxes should go. Before the moving company leaves please register everything and confirm that you are happy with work and everything is perfect.
  • In your car keep all your documents, overnight bag, fragile boxes, and other important items with you.
  • If you have a pet so keep its water, food, and bed with you, if you are directly taking them into your new home with you.
  • Keep photos of bills like water, gas, and electricity meter reading with you whether of your new as well as your old home.

IN THE NEW HOME

Finally, you and your family are at your new home after preparing for a month.

  • We are done with a move.
  • Now first unboxing of essential items box
  • Plug your freezer, fridges, and other large appliances as well as your mobile phone and telephone.
  • Don't hurry in unpacking all the items at once. First, clean the house and make a bed.
  • Get familiarized with the heating system and boiler.
  • For your pet arrange their foods, beds, and blankets from the old home which are similar to them so they can be easily adjusted in a new home.
  • Keep the documents safely in a new home.
  • Through Deliveroo order a takeaway and then yes congratulation to you.
