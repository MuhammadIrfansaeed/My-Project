Following government COVID-19 guidelines, we are fully operational

Removal firms company can perform their work during this pandemic but under the guidelines given for protection from COVID-19 given by the official government, our talented employees are very aware of safety and know how to operate the work and communicate with the customers.

Our priority is the safety of our employees as well as our customers.

What we are doing as a business

Pre-move survey

  • Wherever it is feasible we will survey with the help of video technology.

If an on-site survey is required

  • Our survey team will wear gloves and carry the face mask and discard then discard it after use wherever feasible.
  • Social distance of at least 2m apart will be maintained by our team members.

The move

  • Our team will wear gloves and carry the face mask and discard then discard it after use wherever feasible.
  • Social distance of at least 2m apart will be maintained by our team members to the customers during the removal but it may be difficult for them to maintain social distance from each other.
  • The crew will work together and will maintain the same pairing so it will be easy for you to communicate with them and we try our best to keep the staff as minimum as possible and give them multiple work.
  • You don't worry about covid as we will check all our employees regularly including their temperature and keep the records. in any case, if covid symptoms occur in any employee we will send them back home immediately.
  • Hand sanitizer gels are present in every van and all team members are requested to sanitize their hands daily.
  • We will memorize our team to not touch their faces, sanitize their hand, and do respiratory hygiene. We also memorized them not to sneeze or cough without tissue paper and discard tissue paper after use with responsibility.
  • We will confirm that the window should be open slightly during traveling in a van in the house removal process.
  • Cleaning of a vehicle such as steering wheel, door handles, seats, and dashboards will be done on regular basis, and then load areas with all equipment.

What we ask of our customers

Pre-move survey

In case the on-site survey is needed

  • As a precaution, if any client has a high chance to get infected so that person should not be on-site.
  • When our team reaches for a survey we request our customers to please wear a face mask.
  • When our team reaches for a survey we request our customers be careful that no pets are roaming in the house.
  • When our team reaches for a survey we request our customers that one person from the family will be there. The rest of the other family members are requested to isolate themselves and locked the door of the room and open the window slightly.

The move

  • For the move, we are requested our customers to please deep clean their homes and other items.
  • When our team reaches for help in the move we request our customers that one person from the family will be there and maintain 2m social distance from our team members. The rest of the other family members are requested to isolate themselves and locked the door of the room and open the window slightly.
  • When our team reaches for a removal process we request our customers to please wear a face mask
  • When our team reaches for a removal process we request our customers be careful that no pets are roaming in the house.
  • When our team reaches for a removal process we request our customers that no other tradespeople be in the location.
  • When our team reaches for a removal process we request our customers to provide them W/C and washing facilities and this can only be used by our team members.
